About the Club

The Chess Club is a vibrant community of students passionate about the game of chess. Whether you're a seasoned player or just beginning to explore the world of pawns and knights, our club offers a friendly and inclusive space to learn, play, and grow.

We regularly organize casual games, internal tournaments, workshops, and inter-college competitions to promote strategic thinking and sportsmanship. Our mission is to foster critical thinking, patience, and problem-solving skills through the timeless game of chess.

Join us to sharpen your mind, make new friends, and be part of a community that values both competition and camaraderie.
