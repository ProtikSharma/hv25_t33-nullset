About the Club

The Computer Engineers Society (CES) is the official student body representing the vibrant and ever-evolving community of computer engineering enthusiasts at Siliguri Institute of Technology Founded with the vision of fostering innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence, CES serves as a platform for students to explore the world of computing beyond the classroom. We are a dynamic group of students driven by a shared passion for technology, programming, and problem-solving. Through a variety of workshops, coding competitions, tech talks, and collaborative projects, CES aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application. Our society also promotes peer learning, encourages research initiatives, and helps students stay updated with the latest trends in the tech industry. Whether you’re a beginner curious about coding or a seasoned developer looking to take on new challenges, CES offers opportunities for growth, networking, and innovation. Join us in shaping the future—one line of code at a time!
